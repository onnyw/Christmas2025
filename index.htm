Merry Christmas 2025


 

 

As we slowed down with our working lives, we found ourselves traveling more this year! We had visited Hong Kong, Northwestern China and Peru. We are so thankful that we live in Australia that remained fairly peaceful and we thank God every day for keeping our family safe and provided!


On continued to work part-time for C&MA and also volunteered as a Religious Instructor. He also kept himself busy playing tennis, ping pong and pickleball. He recently joined a local Ukulele group to perform in various senior homes occasionally. In Mar, we went to Hong Kong in order to renew On’s Hong Kong ID card.

We then joined a tour to travel to Xinjiang and Gansu, the northwestern part of China. It was an eye opening experience to see the development and growth in China! We went to many historical places, like Urumqi, Tianchi, Turpan, Jaohe, Mt Huoyan, Mt Mingsha and Crescent Moon Spring, Mogao Grottoes, Jiayuguan, Zihabgye Rainbow Mt and Lanzhou. It was an unforgettable experience! After we came back to Hong Kong, On met up with some of his relatives whom he grew up with!


Brannie continued to work occasionally as a relief teacher and as a volunteer in RSPCA. In June, we visited Peru. We traveled to Lima and went to Nasca to see the mysteries Nasca lines on a plane! It was amazing! We then traveled to Cusco and saw the Incredible Historic Sanctuary of Machu Picchu!, which was in our bucket list for many years! Finally, we went to Puno and saw the Titicaca Lake!

Both of our sons, Andre and Ryan, were doing well and continued to work full-time as a software engineer and as a graphic designer.

We thank God for keeping us safe and healthy!
We pray that you will find peace and love during this Christmas Season!

Wish you all a Blessed Christmas
and a Peaceful New Year!

On, Brannie, Andre & Ryan
